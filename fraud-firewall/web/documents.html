About These Documents

All Verum Omnis documents are cryptographically sealed and immutable. The Constitution v6.0 Final is the authoritative governance document for all Verum Omnis systems, including the Fraud Firewall, evidence vault, and reporting engines.

Documents are sealed with:

Cryptographic Integrity: SHA-512 fingerprinting
Blockchain Anchoring: Bitcoin via OpenTimestamps
Jurisdiction: Constitutional Court of South Africa
Status: Immutable • Court-Validated • Open to All

Visit verumglobal.foundation for additional resources and regulatory information.
